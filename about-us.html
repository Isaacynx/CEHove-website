About Us

Christ Embassy Hove

Christ Embassy Hove is a vibrant and growing church community located in the heart of Brighton & Hove, United Kingdom. We are a part of the Brentwood Group, under the larger umbrella of Christ Embassy DSP (Digital Satellite Partners) UK Zone 3 — a dynamic zone within the global ministry of Believers' LoveWorld Inc., also known as Christ Embassy.

As an extension of this global vision, Christ Embassy Hove is committed to taking God’s divine presence to the people and nations of the world and demonstrating the character of the Holy Spirit right here in our city. We are a place where lives are transformed, faith is strengthened, and the realities of the divine life in Christ are made known and experienced.

Our mission is to raise generations of men and women who will come into their inheritance in Christ, fulfilling God’s dream and purpose for their lives. Through the teaching of God’s Word, times of deep fellowship, prayer, worship, and community outreach, we are impacting lives with the Gospel and equipping believers to live victoriously every day.

At Christ Embassy Hove, every service, program, and initiative is designed to build up the individual, strengthen families, and create an environment where God's love and truth are evident. Whether you are new to faith or have walked with God for years, there is a place for you here to grow, serve, and thrive.

We invite you to be a part of this extraordinary journey of faith, purpose, and love.
