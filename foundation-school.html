Foundation School

What is foundation school?

The aim of the Foundation School is to educate members concerning the basic doctrines of Christ; and lay a good and strong foundation upon which you can build a successful and fulfilling Christian life.

The Foundation School will:
1. Through the manifestation of the Word and by the Holy Spirit, work to present every person perfect in Christ Jesus ( Colossians 1:28 "Whom we preach, warning every man, and teaching every man in all wisdom; that we may present every man perfect in Christ Jesus" )

2. Correct any errors and or false ideas that you might have had prior to this time ( Ephesians 4:11-14 "And he gave some, apostles; and some, prophets; and some, evangelists; and some, pastors and teachers; For the perfecting of the saints, for the work of the ministry, for the edifying of the body of Christ: Till we all come in the unity of the faith, and of the knowledge of the Son of God, unto a perfect man, unto the measure of the stature of the fulness of Christ: That we henceforth be no more children, tossed to and fro, and carried about with every wind of doctrine, by the sleight of men, and cunning craftiness, whereby they lie in wait to deceive;" )

3. Enable you to grow, by increasing in knowledge of God’s Word ( 1 Peter 2:2 "As newborn babes, desire the sincere milk of the word, that ye may grow thereby:" )

4. Prepare you for the ministry of reconciliation. You will be trained to be a master soul winner. ( 2 Corinthians 5:18-19 "And all things are of God, who hath reconciled us to himself by Jesus Christ, and hath given to us the ministry of reconciliation; To wit, that God was in Christ, reconciling the world unto himself, not imputing their trespasses unto them; and hath committed unto us the word of reconciliation." )

How long is foundation school?

The Foundation School modules have been designed to span across 7 lessons. After successfully completing the Foundation School exam, students will graduate at a lovely and colorful ceremony hosted every quarter.

How do I join foundation school?

To join foundation school, please contact Pastor or a leader via Kingschat or after service.
