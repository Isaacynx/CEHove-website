Join a Service Department

“I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of God, that ye present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service.”(Romans 12:1)

At Christ Embassy, every member has a special place and purpose in the house of God. Serving in a department is a meaningful way to grow spiritually, and use your unique gifts to support the work of the ministry. Our service departments are the heartbeat of the church — from ushering and choir to media, outreach, and technical teams, each one plays a vital role in fulfilling our mission to take God’s divine presence to the nations.

The only requirement to join a department is the successful completion of Foundation School. Once you’ve graduated, you’re welcome to join any department that aligns with your passion and calling. Step forward today and become an active part of what God is doing through His church!

Departments

Technical Department

Supports the ministry through excellence in technology and operations. Handles sound, projection, microphones, instruments, and all electronic equipment—ensuring every service runs smoothly and distraction-free, helping the Word reach God’s people clearly.

Choir Department

Ministers to the Lord and the congregation through Spirit-filled songs of worship and praise. The choir leads the church in exalting God, preparing hearts to receive His Word, and creating an atmosphere for the move of the Spirit.

Decorations Department

Beautifies the house of God with creativity and excellence. Through inspired designs and setups, this department creates an atmosphere that reflects the glory, order, and beauty of the Kingdom.

Ushering Department

Serves as the welcoming arm of the church, ensuring order, warmth, and excellence in every service. Ushers assist members and visitors, provide direction, and support the smooth flow of all church activities with a spirit of love and dedication.
